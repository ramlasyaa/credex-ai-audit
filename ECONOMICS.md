# Unit Economics & ROI

If Credex deploys this tool, here is the economic breakdown based on a B2B SaaS lead-generation model.

**1. Value of a Converted Lead to Credex (LTV)**
Assumption: Credex sells discounted AI credits (e.g., a 15% discount on a $20k annual contract, where Credex takes a 5% margin).
- Average Startup AI Spend: $1,500/month ($18,000/year).
- Credex Margin: ~5% of total contract volume.
- Credex Revenue per customer: ~$900/year.
- Assuming an average lifespan of 2 years, the **LTV of a converted customer is $1,800**.

**2. CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost) via GTM Plan**
Since the GTM relies heavily on organic channels (Reddit, X threads, Accounting Partnerships), the primary cost is the time/salary of the person executing it + the Anthropic API cost per audit.
- API cost per audit: ~$0.01 (Anthropic Haiku).
- Time cost for distribution: ~$500/week (internal labor).
- If we get 250 audits per week, the "fully loaded" cost per audit is ~$2.01.
- Assuming a 2% end-to-end conversion rate from audit to signed contract, it takes 50 audits to get 1 customer.
- **CAC = $100.50** (50 * $2.01).

**3. Required Conversion Rates for Profitability**
With an LTV of $1,800 and an organic CAC of $100.50, the unit economics are phenomenally profitable (LTV:CAC ratio of ~18:1). 
Even if we introduce paid ads at a CPC of $2.00:
- 100 clicks = $200
- 20% complete audit = 20 audits
- 10% book consultation = 2 leads
- 25% close rate = 0.5 customers
- Paid CAC = $400. 
This remains highly profitable.

**4. The Path to $1M ARR in 18 Months**
To reach $1M in Annual Recurring Revenue, assuming $900 ARR per customer, Credex needs **1,111 active customers**.
Over 18 months, that requires acquiring ~62 customers per month.
Working backward through the funnel:
- 25% close rate on consultations = 248 consultations needed per month.
- 10% of audited users book a consultation = 2,480 completed audits needed per month.
- 20% of landing page visitors complete an audit = **12,400 unique visitors needed per month**.

To achieve 12.4k visitors/month, the tool must move beyond manual outreach and rank organically for terms like "Cursor vs Copilot cost" or be embedded as a widget in popular developer blogs.
