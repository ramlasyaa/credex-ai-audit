# GTM Strategy: AI Spend Auditor

**Target User**
The exact target user is a "Head of Engineering" or "CTO" at a Seed or Series A startup (10-50 employees). At this stage, they are personally approving software expenses but don't have a dedicated procurement team, meaning AI spend is likely decentralized and poorly tracked.

**The Trigger (Right before they want this)**
They just received their monthly AWS/Stripe invoice and noticed the "Software Subscriptions" line item has ballooned by 30% month-over-month, primarily due to developers expensing individual Cursor, ChatGPT, and Claude subscriptions.

**Where they hang out online**
1. **Specific Subreddits:** r/SaaS, r/startup, r/devops.
2. **Slack Communities:** Y Combinator alumni channels, CTO Craft, Rands Leadership Slack.
3. **X (Twitter) Lists:** VCs discussing burn rates, "build in public" founders.

**First 100 Users in 30 Days ($0 Budget)**
I will run a highly targeted outbound campaign on X and LinkedIn called "The AI Stack Roast."
1. I will search X for founders complaining about Vercel, OpenAI, or SaaS bills (using advanced search: `"OpenAI bill" OR "SaaS spend" min_faves:5`).
2. I will reply with a screenshot of the Audit Tool showing a mock calculation of a similar company saving $800/mo.
3. I will post a thread in r/SaaS titled: *"I analyzed 50 startup AI stacks. 80% of you are wasting $500+/mo on individual ChatGPT Plus seats when you should be using API direct. Here's a free calculator to check your stack."*

**The Unfair Distribution Channel**
Credex already has relationships with startups. We can partner with Boutique Accounting Firms (like Pilot or Kruze Consulting). These accountants see the raw SaaS spend of startups. We give them a co-branded version of this Audit Tool to send to their clients as a "value-add cost-saving report." The accountants look like heroes, and Credex gets high-intent, pre-qualified leads.

**Week-1 Traction Metrics**
If successful, Week 1 looks like:
- 1,500 unique visitors
- 250 completed audits
- 40 emails captured (16% conversion rate on value-gate)
- 5 high-value consultations booked with Credex.
